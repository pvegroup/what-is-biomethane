Key Takeaways

  • Biomethane is upgraded biogas with over 95% methane content, matching natural gas in energy value but with carbon-neutral impact.
  • Thailand, Indonesia, and Malaysia are adopting biomethane as a clean energy alternative for transportation, grid injection, and industrial applications.
  • Biomethane is produced through anaerobic digestion of organic waste (food scraps, agricultural residues, manure) followed by upgrading processes.
  • Most diesel engines can be converted to run on biomethane with payback periods of 12-24 months through fuel savings.
  • Biomethane supports circular economy practices by turning waste into valuable renewable energy.
